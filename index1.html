Reliability

Reliability - the property of an object to keep in time within the established limits the values ​​of all parameters characterizing the ability to perform the required functions in the given conditions of use, maintenance, storage and transportation.

Compactness

A compact space is a certain type of topological spaces that generalizes the properties of boundedness and closedness in Euclidean spaces to arbitrary topological spaces.

In general topology, compact spaces in their properties resemble finite sets in set theory.
